Dragon Servant Light
Effect
Light
Lv 4
Dragon
500/2000

You can Special Summon this card (from your hand) by banishing 1 "Dragon King" monster from your GY. If Summoned this way: you can target 1 of your banished Dragon monster, return it to the GY, and if you do, you can banish 
1 Dragon monster with a different name from your graveyard. If this card is destroyed by a card effect, you can destroy 1 "Dragon Servant" monster from your deck. You can only use each effect of "Dragon King Light"

Dragon Servant Void 
Effect
Dark
Lv 4
Dragon
1800/700

You can Special Summon this card (from your hand) by banishing 1 "Dragon King" monster from your GY. If Summoned this way: you can target 1 Dragon Type monster you control, it is treated as a tuner monster for the rest of

Dragon King Wish 
Spell

At the start of your Main Phase 1: Draw 2 cards, also, for the rest of this duel after you activate this card, you cannot activate Spell/Trap Cards or effects, except "Dragon King" Spell/Trap Cards or effects. 
You can banish this card from your GY; reveal 1 "Dragon" monster in your hand, shuffle it into the Deck, then,  add 1 "Dragon King" or "Dragon Servant" Monster from your deck to your hand. 
You can only use each effect of "Dragon King Wish" once per turn.
